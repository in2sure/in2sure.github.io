Our Services

We offer services to both crypto and non-crypto business that utilize distributed technologies.

Business Model Designs

Today’s global risks and challenges call for innovative solutions. Our team leverages leading academic research to deliver novel technology solutions for data analysis and insurance model designs. We offer the mathematical core of risk-sharing schemes that bridge the gap between insurance clients and investors. The design of risk-sharing schemes can be tailored to deal with different types of risks and to meet the needs of investors with varying risk appetite and financial capacity. Such models can mix and match features of business models like takaful, peer-to-peer insurance, catastrophe risk pooling, traditional insurance, etc.

Independent Review and Model Audits

Many whitepapers for DeFi protocols are difficult to understand, even for professionally trained analysts. Technical components for tokenomics often need more scientific rigor and sound methodologies, making them difficult to gain public trust. Our tailor-made review report can offer mathematical formulations and a holistic overview of its core technical foundation. While most DeFi protocols go through security audits today, model risks are much harder to detect without logical analysis and extensive simulations. We provide thorough scientific analysis as an independent third party in a similar manner to the peer-review process in academic publications. The reviews show our level of confidence in the protocol’s long-term viability for meeting its financial obligations.

Reserve and Capital Management

Reserves and capitals are critical components of the insurance business. We can help innovative small businesses to perform cash flow analysis and stress testing. Credentialed actuaries in our network can be commissioned to help calculate reserves and capitals in compliance with regulations from different jurisdictions.
