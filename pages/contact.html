Let’s talk

With the advent of modern technologies such as blockchain, artificial intelligence, internet of things, we believe in a more transparent, equitable, and inclusive future of risk management. The future of risk management solutions should also echo the reminiscence of its past as collaborative and self-grouped organizations, such as burial societies and brotherly associations, that provide financial support for members at times of dire needs. From ancient Rome and today’s society, risk management techniques equipped with modern-day technology should continue to serve the needs of the people at every fabric of our society.

Distributed Insurtech Foundation is envisioned to be an open society for technology enthusiasts, educators, and researchers. We hope to contribute to the development of distributed technologies for risk management and help entrepreneurs launch new applications. It is designed to be a repository of education and research materials and a consulting organization that offers services for designing, engineering, and risk analysis of distributed business models and data technologies. The foundation is currently managed by a startup team that works with business partners and distributes work to contributors. The foundation is expected to eventually become a decentralized autonomous organization.

We welcome new business partners and contributors to the foundation. We do not currently accept any donations or offer any grants as it takes time to grow the organization. If you are looking for novel solutions to your business or to contribute to our foundation, please do not hesitate to reach out.
